About Us

Our History

Best Banking was established in 2019 with the aim to promote secured and convenient banking to every customer. As the country's economy grows, a small group of people felt that they had to take up the unique responsibility of financing fledgling industries and coming up with new ones.

True to our mission as a development bank, we focused on helping others grow even while we were a young bank and new player in the banking scene.

Our Mission & Vision

We seek to "Make Banking Safe and Easy" - by leveraging various digital technologies and embedding ourselves seamlessly into our customers' lives to deliver simple while high-quality banking solutions and experiences. We are here for the long term, with a view to enrich lives, transform businesses and drive sustainable outcomes.

We hold ourselves to the highest ethical standards and assume that everything we do is in full public view. Fair dealing is the foundation of our business. That way, the trust in us that was built by our predecessors and us will never be eroded.
