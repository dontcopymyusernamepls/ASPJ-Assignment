Oceanus Cabinet

Creative and beautifully made, the Oceanus Cabinet makes a lasting impression with its striking design that features both refined artistic accents and organic details. Made of solid ash, ash veneer, MDF board, and metal, the Oceanus Cabinet displays impressive craftsmanship and attention to detail. The paint finish enhances the fish scale pattern and it is applied in different hues to create a gradient effect, starting from unfinished ash veneer, to light gray and cool muted blue.
